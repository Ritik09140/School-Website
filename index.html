🏫 The New Achiever Pre-Science School, Limdi

🎯 Board Exams તથા JEE / NEET જેવી Competitive Exams માટે વિશેષ માર્ગદર્શન સાથે સતત ઉત્તમ પરિણામો પ્રાપ્ત થાય છે.

🌱 અમે શિક્ષણ સાથે સંસ્કાર, આત્મવિશ્વાસ અને ઉજ્જવળ ભવિષ્ય માટે વિદ્યાર્થીઓને તૈયાર કરીએ છીએ.

🏅 Presidential Honor — A Moment of National Pride

Proud of Our School 🇮🇳

In a truly historic and unforgettable moment, the students and faculty of The New Achiever Pre-Science School, Limdi were blessed with a personal audience with the Honourable Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi Ji.

This extraordinary recognition stands as a testament to the school's dedication to academic excellence, science education, and the holistic growth of every student in rural Gujarat. To be acknowledged at the highest national level fills every heart in our school family with immense pride and joy.

"We do not just teach science — we build the future of our nation, one student at a time. This honor from the Prime Minister is not ours alone — it belongs to every child who dared to dream."
